The chairman and members of the (BoC) of the Independent High Electoral Commission in Iraq (IHEC), held their sixth regular meeting on 21/2/2023 to discuss topics of the agenda, including the belongings of the candidates of the Provincial councils and parliamentary councils for the previous years, to cancel the accreditation of the candidacy of candidates who are covered by the accountability and justice procedures per the official letters submitted to IHEC from the relevant authorities. In light of developing the work of IHEC, the Board of Commissioners is working hard to communicate with international organizations supporting the democratic process in Iraq, so the chairman of the board of commissioners discussed with the regional director of the International Foundation for electoral systems (IFES) the mechanism of collective work at the level of the institution; to develop a positive interaction in the success of the work of IHEC in its administrative and technical aspects, through joint and diligent work that meets the aspirations of the next stage. IHEC is keening to maintain continuous communication with its stakeholders, including non-governmental organizations with electoral significance, because of the important role it plays in enhancing the confidence of voters and urging them to participate in the elections, so the chairman of the board of commissioners discussed with representatives of the ( coalition of Election Observation organizations in Iraq) , the visions and ideas that would achieve common goals in the upcoming electoral benefits. As part of the field follow-up, the members of the board of Commissioners, the Directors Generals, and the managers of the relevant departments pursued the work of the electoral offices in both governorates, Basra and Holy Karbala, to evaluate their performance technically and administratively, and to indicate the extent of their obligation to the directives and instructions issued by IHEC , as well as follow-up on the achievements’ rates in both processes, biometric registration and distribution of voter cards.
